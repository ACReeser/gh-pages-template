Sniper's Aim
Gunner Feature
At-Will * Martial
Move Action                 Ranged sight
Target: One creature
Effect: Mark the creature. Gain +2 to ranged attack rolls against the marked target and -2 to ranged attack rolls against non-marked target, until the end of your turn.   The creature remains marked until it moves out of sight or you dismiss the mark as a free action.
Sustain Move: Gain +3 to ranged attack rolls against the marked target and -2 to ranged attack rolls against non-marked targets, until the end of your turn.
