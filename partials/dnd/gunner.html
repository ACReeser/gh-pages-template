SPECIALIZATIONS
A Gunner can choose one specialization. Presented here are Marksman, Trencher, and Alchemical Arbalest.

The following are suggested feats for all Gunners:
Heroic feats: Weapon Focus [Crossbows] (+1 damage, increasing), Speed Loader (free action to load minor), Distant Damage (can flank with ranged)
Paragon feats: Grazing Shot (Dex mod damage on ranged miss)
Epic feats: Deft Aim (Target REF with basic ranged)
    Marksman: Some Gunners train endlessly to achieve supreme marksmanship.   Hour after hour, from shooting ranges to archery tournaments, Marksmen strive for the perfect shot. Marksmen are the best at the back of the party, focusing on individuals and picking them off one by one.

Marksman: Gain the Sniper's Aim feature

Suggested Attachments:
Spyglass

Suggested Heroic Feats: Far Shot (+5 to normal, long range w/bows), Grounding Shot (No penalty for targeting prone targets), Low Crawl (can shift while prone)
Suggested Paragon Feats: Overwhelming Critical (knock prone on crit), Steady Shooter (+3 damage with crossbow if you don't move)
Suggested Epic Feats: Bow Mastery (Crit on 19-20 with ranged weapon)
    Trencher: A down and dirty crossbowmen, Trencher's aren't afraid to get up close and personal with targets. Utilizing the crossbow's close-range penetrating power and their own close-quarter combat drill, Trenchers are best when dealing with rows of enemies at close range.   Trencher tactics are said to have originated with dwarven tunnel-fighting, and their drill reflects this.

Trencher: Gain the Point Blank Shooter feature.

Suggested Attachments:
Springblades
Firelance

Suggested Heroic feats: Quickdraw (draw as part of an attack), Sidearm Attachment [NEW] (Can use Hand Crossbow as a slingrail attachment. See Sidearm Shot power)
    Alchemical Arbalest: In order to augment their bolts, Alchemical Arbalests turn to flasks and vials of volatile ingredients.   Alchemical Arbalests are a new innovation: their mastery over elemental damage makes them potent enemies, especially when pitted against groups of enemies. However, their chosen discipline is not a cheap one: alchemical items are expensive and reagent crafting takes time.

Alchemical Arbalest: Add your Wisdom modifier to damage rolls made with alchemical items.

Suggested Attachments:
Alchemical Mortar

Suggested Feats:
Alchemist (can craft alchemical items),
Field Alchemist [NEW] (prereq Alchemical Arbalest Class Feature)- Alchemical creation time is cut in half.

ACCESSORIST
All Gunners share an obsession with their weapon (or weapons) of choice.Whether it's the stealthy Hand Crossbow, the intimidating Pump Action Double Crossbow, or the efficient Autospring Crossbow, Gunners tinker endlessly with their killing machine.

A Gunner's choice in weaponry may drastically change which powers he or she uses. Attachments can be switched out during a short or extended rest.   Note that some powers require a specific base weapon or attachment in order to function.

Gunners have access to a variety of base weapons and attachments with which to wield.   
One-handed base weapons (Upperrail):
Hand Crossbow- +2 prof., 1d6 damage, 10/20 range, cost 25 gp. Load free.   Upperrail. See "Sidearm Attachment" feat
Two-handed base weapons (Upperrail and slingrail):
Light Crossbow- +2 prof., 1d8 damage, 15/30 range, cost 25gp. Load minor.
Pump Action Double Crossbow- +1 prof., 2d6 damage, 10/20 range, cost 40gp. Load minor.
Heavy Crossbow- +2 prof. 1d10 damage, 15/30 range, cost 50gp. Load move.
Autospring Crossbow- +2 prof. 1d10 damage, 15/35 range, cost 100gp. Magazine 6, Load free. Load move to replace magazine.

Attachments:
Spyglass- Prereq: Upperrail.   Cost 50gp.   Gain +2 to attack rolls when target is at long range.
Springblades- Prereq: Slingrail, two-handed. Cost 25gp. Gain a standard melee attack: STR vs AC, 1d6 + STR
Thunderbolt Thrower- Cost 75gp. Gain a basic ranged attack: DEX vs REF, 1d8 + WIS. This can only be used once per round.
Alchemical Mortar- Prereq: Slingrail. Cost 75gp. Fire Volatile alchemical items with a +2 proficiency bonus to attack rolls. Load minor.
Firelance- Prereq: Slingrail. Cost 20gp. Consumable. Close blast 2, DEX + 2 vs REF, 1d4 Fire damage + 1d4 Force.
Grappling Bolt- Cost 5gp.   Can fire a grappling hook up to 50 ft.
