About me

Game Dev Interests

Video games have held my fascination since I played Civ I and SimAnt as a kid. In high school I started programming games in ancient QBASIC. I made half a dozen sprite-based games, but unfortunately, I don't have any of the art or code anymore. In college I learned proper programming, and after college I learned some web design. I am most intrigued by games with interactive mechanics, meta-game references, and asynchronous or irregular presentation.
